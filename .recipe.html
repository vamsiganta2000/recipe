Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings (e.g. pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers)

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add cheese and toppings.
  5. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until crust is golden brown.
  6. Slice and serve hot.
